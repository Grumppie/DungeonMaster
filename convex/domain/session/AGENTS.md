## Session Domain

Owns room/session lifecycle, public/private room policy, host transfer, participant presence, readiness, and join visibility rules.

Does not own adventure generation, combat legality, DM replies, or voice playback.

Add future changes here when they affect:
- room creation or join
- participant projection
- host moderation
- room cleanup or presence

Do not add:
- combat mutations
- scene generation
- DM narration logic
